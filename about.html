What is SkillSwap 2.0?

SkillSwap 2.0 is a community-driven skill exchange platform where people learn and teach without money. Instead of traditional payments, teachers earn SkillCoins when they teach ‚Äî and learners use SkillCoins to enroll in new skills. The platform uses AI recommendations to match learners and teachers based on interests and proficiency levels. Our focus is growth, collaboration, and equal access to opportunities.

  • Community-driven, peer-to-peer learning
  • Earn SkillCoins by teaching
  • Use SkillCoins to learn new skills
  • AI matching to connect the right people
  • Built for growth and collaboration

Our Mission

To make learning accessible to everyone by creating a global ecosystem of skill sharing that empowers individuals, removes financial barriers, and fosters real human connections. We aim to make learning mutual, rewarding, and community-centered.

  • Make learning accessible
  • Create a global skill-sharing network
  • Empower individuals through teaching
  • Remove financial barriers from self-improvement
  • Promote community growth and real connections
